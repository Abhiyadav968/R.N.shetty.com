Courses offered

R.N.Shetty Pre-University College, Kundapura is a private unaided college affiliated to the Karnataka State Pre-university Board, Bengaluru. The medium of instruction is English. The college admits both boys and girls based on their performance in 10th standard examination.


The college offers the two year Pre-University course with the following subject combinations.

Group I: Languages

  1. English
  2. Kannada/ Hindi/ Sanskrit

Group II : Science stream

  1. Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics, Biology (PCMB)
  2. Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics, Computer Science (PCMCs)
  3. Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics, Statistics (PCMS)
  4. Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics, Electronics (PCME)

Group II: Commerce stream

  1. Economics, Business Studies, Accountancy & Statistics (EBAS)
  2. Economics, Business Studies, Accountancy & Compter Science (EBAC)
  3. Economics, Business Studies, Accountancy & Basic Maths (EBAB)

Other Special Courses:

  • KCET/JEE/NEET Training
  • KCET Crash Course
  • CA-CPT Training for Commerce Students
